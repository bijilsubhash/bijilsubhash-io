Hands-On with dbt Agent Skills February 7, 2026

Earlier last week, dbt agent skills were released to augment the behaviour of your coding agents to follow some of the best practices that is expected of a collaborator in your dbt project. I have been using agents for coding at some capacity with mixed results, and truthfully given the amount of dbt work I engage in, I was waiting for some opinionated framework that can be used to enhance this new way of working. That’s why when dbt labs announced the reased last week, I was quite eager to try it out and share some of my early findings. I am not going to delve into the details of what is included in the release, which you can read in detail here and instead focus on some of the result that came out of the experiment.
